WHO WE ARE

We were founded in 1990, with a heart for fellowship with the Lord and His children.

Our name is birthed from the scripture John 15:15, “No longer do I call you slaves, for the slave does not know what his master is doing; but I have called you friends…” (NASB). We believe that we have been called to abide (John 15:4) and be in fellowship with God (1 Cor 1:8), and it is through this reconciliation that we can bear witness of His love in a communal way.

Paul petitioned a slave master, Philemon, to accept his once slave, Onesimus. He believed the Good News of Jesus changed the relationship between slave and master. It is through our changed relationship with the Lord that we can have hope and bear His image.

We are a body in partnership together to love each other and honor God.

OUR MISSION

Display Jesus, Love People, Make Disciples, and Rescue A Generation.
